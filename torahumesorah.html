Torah Umesorah

Voitex's enabled Torah
Umesorah to offer offline
educational materials.

Background:

Torah Umesorah, a leading organization in chinuch, sought to extend the reach of its educational materials beyond its online platform. As a comprehensive resource covering various chinuch topics, including education development, Torah Umesorah recognized the need for a scalable solution to cater to a wider audience.

Challenges

The organization faced several challenges in its quest to distribute educational content effectively. Despite having an established online platform for teachers and principals, Torah Umesorah recognized the importance of reaching individuals offline. Additionally, they needed a system that could accommodate self enrollment, diverse content types and provide seamless user experience.

Solutions

Voitex emerged as the ideal partner to address Torah Umesorah s challenges. Leveraging Voitex s innovative phone based system, Torah Umesorah was able to provide offline access to its educational materials. Teachers and principals could easily access classes by calling in, setting up memberships, and selecting content based on their interests. Voitex s system categorized content by grade level, topics, presenters, and learning levels, ensuring accessibility and relevance for all users.

Additional Utilization

In addition to providing access to educational materials, Torah Umesorah utilized Voitex s features for live teleconferences and automated registration processes. Furthermore, Torah Umesorah leveraged Voitex s reporting options to gain valuable insights into class popularity, duration, and other metrics.

The Result

The partnership with Voitex enabled Torah Umesorah to achieve its goals of expanding educational distribution and enhancing user experience. Teachers and principals now have seamless access to a wide range of educational content, regardless of their internet connectivity. The organization has seen increased engagement and satisfaction among users, ultimately leading to greater impact and effectiveness in chinuch initiatives.
