About Us

User-friendly, no-code IVR & VoIP solutions for leading hotlines and businesses, offering utmost flexibility and superior service.

Voitex was founded by Joel Gancz, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for advancing communication technology and helping individuals reach their communication goals.

Together with a close-knit community of customers, Voitex has evolved into a multichannel platform, offering comprehensive communication solutions tailored to diverse needs. Innovation is at the heart of Voitex, as we continually adapt to meet the evolving requirements of our valued customers.

The Voitex Team

Our dedicated team comprises highly talented individuals who share a common goal: to make communication dreams a reality. From our development team to customer service and office administration, every member is committed to delivering unparalleled customer satisfaction.
